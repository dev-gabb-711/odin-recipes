Triple-Dipped Fried Chicken

Description

This fried chicken batter yields the crispiest, spiciest, homemade fried chicken I have ever tasted!
It has been a picnic favorite in my family for years and tastes equally good served hot or cold.

The crispiest fried chicken is just a few ingredients away when you use this top-rated fried chicken batter!

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Either bone-in or boneless skin-on chicken (parts may vary)
  • Oil: Preferably vegetable or peanut oil
  • Seasoned Flour
    • All-Purpose Flour
    • Garlic Powder
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper (Freshly Crushed)
    • Paprika
    • Poultry Seasoning
  • Beer Batter
    • Beer
    • All-Purpose Flour
    • Egg yolks
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients of the seasoned flour to create the mixture.
  2. Mix all the ingredients to create the beer batter.
  3. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk for a few hours.
  4. Once done marinating, coat the chicken in the seasoned flour.
  5. Then dip the chicken in the beer batter and dip it again in the seasoned flour.
  6. Ensure that you remove any excess
  7. Fry the chicken in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
